Sabre

How does Sabre work?

Sabre is the fastest of the fencing weapons and uses the priority rule. Sabre is also the only fencing sword that the fencer uses slashes and not stabs to score points on the opponent. The target area of a sabre fencer is anywhere from the waist and up excluding the hands.
Sabre is the second fastest olympic sport only second behind rifle shooting.
