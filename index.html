What is Fencing

Fencing is a fun and exciting sport that is one of five sports that has been in every summer olympics. The sport involves two fencers who try to hit their opponents with their swords. Every time the opponent is hit, the referee rewards the attacker with a point. The game 'Bout' is over when 15 points are reached by one of the fencers or the time is up.

Fencing is split into three different disiplines which are almost different sports. All three involve trying to hit the opponent with one's weapon but thats about all they have in common. The three disiplines are named after the weapons they use with Sabre being the fastest, Foil being the most technical, and Épée being the slowest.
